Understanding Padding, Border, and Margin

Content-Box Model (Default)

In the content-box model, the `width` property only includes the content. Padding, border, and margin are added outside of the content width.

Content-Box Example
(Width: 300px)

Border-Box Model

In the border-box model, the `width` property includes the content, padding, and border. Padding and border do not add extra width.

Border-Box Example
(Width: 300px)

Padding Example

Padding adds space inside the element, between the content and the border.

Margin Example

Margin adds space outside the element, separating it from other elements.
